Bens grannys homemade oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 granny
  • 1 pound of lemon seasoning
  • 70 tons of old bay
  • 1 year of suffering
  • 1 oven that goes to 1000 celcius

steps

  1. preheat the oven to 1000 celcius
  2. put granny in the oven
  3. then give her a slap
  4. pour the 1 pound of lemon seasoning onto her
  5. pour the 70 tons of old bay onto her
  6. go through 1 year of eternal suffering
